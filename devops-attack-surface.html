Why Target DevOps?

DevOps platforms are target-rich environments with high-level permissions for managing credentials, creating infrastructure, configuring systems, building code, and deploying applications to production. In a security landscape that places heavy scrutiny on the endpoint, DevOps systems provide an opportunity to live off the land and escalate privileges while maintaining operational security.
